About DharmaChat

The story behind the platform

"The eternal wisdom of Sanatana Dharma belongs to every seeker — not just scholars. DharmaChat was built to make that wisdom accessible, honest, and alive for everyone."

Our Mission

DharmaChat exists to bring the sacred wisdom of Hindu scripture to everyday life — clearly, authentically, and without barriers.

We believe the Bhagavad Gita, the Upanishads, the Ramayana, the Mahabharata, and the Puranas contain answers to every question a human being will ever face. Our mission is to make that wisdom searchable, conversational, and deeply personal.

Every answer our AI gives is grounded exclusively in authentic Hindu scripture — with full shloka references, Sanskrit verses, transliteration, and practical application. No opinion. No guesswork. Only dharma.

What We Build

  • DharmaAI — An AI sage trained on 18+ sacred Hindu scriptures, answering questions about dharma, karma, moksha, and life through the lens of authentic verse.
  • Scripture Library — Complete texts of the Bhagavad Gita (all 18 chapters), the Mahabharata (all 18 Parvas), and hundreds of curated shlokas across Vedic, Puranic, and Upanishadic traditions.
  • Dharma Articles — In-depth guides on Hindu philosophy, character studies from the epics, and practical applications of dharmic principles.
  • Guided Sadhana — Daily spiritual practices, shloka of the day, and personalised teachings — available to Premium members.
